What it is

A tasting game with enough structure to make every pour matter.

CaVino is built for groups around a table: players taste, make calls, use chips, and react to what everyone else is doing. It works for casual wine nights, hosted tastings, and shop events where people want a reason to talk about what they are drinking.

Social by design

Easy to enter, built around conversation, and made for groups up to 10 players.

Tasting-first

Reference cards and notes help players observe, compare, and remember each wine.

Event-ready

Good for wine shops, private hosts, and evenings that need a guided activity.

In the box

Everything needed to run a CaVino table.

The first package plan includes printed instructions, a folded play mat, chips for up to 10 players, chip bags, a package case, a dealer chip, and trading chips.

  • Game instructions
  • Folded play mat
  • 30 chips for 10 players
  • Container bags
  • Package case
  • Dealer chip
  • Trading chips

For shops and hosts

Bring CaVino to a tasting night.

CaVino can be introduced as a hosted wine-shop activity, with the game explained at the table, selected wines prepared in advance, and a simple feedback or interest list gathered after the session.
